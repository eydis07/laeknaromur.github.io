Læknarómur

Automating transcriptions of radiology dictations in Icelandic to reduce delays and the workload of radiologists.

About Læknarómur

Læknarómur was founded to improve health services by increasing automation and the part computers play in diagnosing patients.

With the aid of speech recognizers and clinical language technology computers can transcribe and understand radiology dictations in real-time.

This software is currently in development.
