About Læknarómur

Læknarómur was founded to improve health services by increasing automation and the part computers play in diagnosing patients.

With the aid of speech recognizors, computers can now listen to radiology dictations in e.g. Icelandic realtime. This in turn can speed up diagnoses, suggest diagnoses by looking up previous ones and more.

This software is currently in development.
